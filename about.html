About

Our mission is to create a safe, collaborative, and fun experience for Tufts University students and community members who want to learn a new language! We believe the best way to do exactly that is to get the community talking with each other in as many langauges as possible. Our service is simple. Sign up, and get an immediate response from our servers of possible candidates who are willing to chat 1-on-1 in the language of your choice. Want to learn French? Easy! Just sign up, put in French as your requested language and reach out to as many or as few candidates and start mastering as soon as later that day.
